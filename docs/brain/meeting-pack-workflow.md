# Meeting Pack Workflow

Meeting packs support preparation and recap.

## Prep Pack

Includes:

- goal
- known context
- people and organizations
- questions to ask
- risks
- documents to request
- stress support tip when opted in

## Recap Pack

Requires a saved session. Includes:

- transcript-based summary
- explicit decisions when detected
- proposed commitments
- follow-ups
- missing info
- draft follow-up text

Drafts are never sent automatically.
